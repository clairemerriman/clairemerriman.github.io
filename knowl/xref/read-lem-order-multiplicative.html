Lemma 116. Lemma 10.3.5.

Let \(n\in\N,\) and let \(\overline{a},\overline{b}\in\Z_n\) be reduced residues (see: reduced residue system modulo \(m\)). If \(\ord_n a\) and \(\ord_n b\) are relatively prime, then
\begin{equation*} \ord_n (ab)=(\ord_n a)(\ord_n b). \end{equation*}

Proof.
