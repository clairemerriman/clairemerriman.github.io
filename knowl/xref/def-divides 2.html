Definition 7. \(a\) divides \(b\).

Let \(a,b\in \Z\text{.}\) Then \(a\) divides \(b\), denoted \(a\mid b\text{,}\) if there exists an integer \(c\) such that \(b=ac\text{.}\) If \(a\mid b\text{,}\) then \(a\) is said to be a divisor or factor of \(b\). The notation \(a\nmid b\) means \(a\) does not divide \(b\text{.}\)
