Problem 173. Chapter 5, Exercise 5.

  1. Let \(m\) be a positive integer and let \(a\) and \(b\) be integers relatively prime to \(m\) with \((\ord_m a, \ord_m b)=1\text{.}\) Prove that \(\ord_m(ab)=(\ord_m a)(\ord_m b)\text{.}\)
  2. Show that the hypothesis \((\ord_m a, \ord_m b)=1\) cannot be eliminated from part (a). What can be said about \(\ord_m(ab)\) if \((\ord_m a, \ord_m b)\neq1\text{?}\)
